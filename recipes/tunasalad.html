Tuna Salad

Description

Making tuna salad at home is one of the easiest, most satisfying meals you can whip up from pantry ingredients

Ingredients

  • Canned Tuna
  • Mayonnaise
  • Celery
  • Red Onion
  • Parsley
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Drain tuna
  2. Add and mix all the ingredients to the bowl
